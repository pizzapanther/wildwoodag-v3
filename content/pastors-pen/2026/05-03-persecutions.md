Precious in the sight of the Lord is the death of His saints. Psalm 116:15

Believers in North America have suffered very little, if any persecution on any large scale. Yet, in many places around the world it is different. Globally, 1 in 7 Christians face persecution simply for following Jesus. Some 7,000 Christians have been killed in Nigeria this year alone – and 7,800 more abducted! (Voice of the Martyrs).

It is time that we rise up and cry out for our brothers and sisters! Let us lift our voices in prayer for them often, and may we spread the word of these atrocities. Let us also call on our government and the governments around the world to end these persecutions, abductions, and killings.
