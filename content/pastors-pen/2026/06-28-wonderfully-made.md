For by Him all things were created that are in heaven and that are on earth, visible and invisible, whether thrones or dominions or principalities or powers. All things were created through Him for Him. Colossians 1:16

You and I are God’s handiwork! He is the Potter; we are the clay.  We know this is true, at least we should.

Today, everyone walks around with a smart phone. Perhaps you have an android made by Samsung, or maybe you have an iPhone made by Apple. I am sure, however, that no one would say “My phone is a product of evolution; it just slimmed out of the ocean.” Why not? Because we know the truth.

Surely you know that you are a wonderful creation, more advanced than any smart phone, If you didn’t know that, I’m telling you now: God, your Creator, loves you and wants to remind you that you are “fearfully and wonderfully made.”
