Set your mind on things above, not on things on the earth. 3 For you died, and your life is hidden with Christ in God. Colossians 3:2-3

I think about faith and realize that people who see what is unseen (Hebrews 11:1) have an advantage. This is important when we learn to live “conscious of heaven and eternity” as Bill Johnson says it. Not only does it change the way we live, but it changes the impact we have on society, especially those around us.
